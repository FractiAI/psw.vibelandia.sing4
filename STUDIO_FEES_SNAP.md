## Canonical rule

**Studio fees** = fees for using the **studio team** and studio-quality production. Artist creator gives executive instruction to his **studio** or concert team. Production WAV (1–12 tracks) · Download · Playlist · Catalog. Copyright-free · Seed:Edge motor only.

| Tier | Plan | Studio fee | What you get |

## Studio vs concert

- **Studio team** — Studio-quality production; same fees as above; select “Studio” in Text-to-Track Motor.
- **Concert team** — Concert-style production; same fee tiers; select “Concert” in Text-to-Track Motor.

Same pricing; team choice (studio or concert) is a creative option, not a fee tier.

## Music Studio · Ultimate Baller V Music Channel

- **Music Studio (Jukebox):** Under School of Music. Access to Text-to-Track Motor, playlist, catalog. Studio fees apply when you generate production WAV (BALLER V PRODUCER tiers above).
- **Ultimate Baller V Music Channel:** Members only (Gold Hearts Club · Ultimate Baller V). Exclusive productions playlist. Studio fees for **new** production = BALLER V PRODUCER.

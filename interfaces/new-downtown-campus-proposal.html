Research · Vibeability · Full connect (NSPFRNP for all)

Every proposal researches the target downtown (geography, decay, assets, demographics, history, hot spots — real names), scores it for vibeability (contribution density potential, walkability, decay vs. potential, safe vibing readiness, hot spots, first people/local narrative, anchor event feasibility), and integrates both into the proposal so the proposal is fully connected to the downtown's reality and hot spots. Downtown Research Summary + Vibeability Score live alongside the one-pager. Include in NSPFRNP for all.

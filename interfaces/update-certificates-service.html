Time-stamped NSPFRNP attestations — capture, delivery, valuation — streamed across all applicable series.

What you get

Update Certificates are time-stamped, NSPFRNP-style attestations that confirm what was delivered, captured, and streamed — like the certificates we've been providing. They come with the advertisement package (Broadcast Pipe Ad Space): your certificate shows proof of broadcast into the EGS cloud (Sun, Earth, 3I/ATLAS triangulation) — El Gran Sol, Earth, 3I/ATLAS mirror — so you have irrefutable proof your ad was broadcast.

Certificate types:

  • CUT! SNAP — All conversation since Action! captured and streamed across all series.
  • Action! SNAP — Live recording confirmation; timestamps kept.
  • Capture tables — What was captured (deliverables, SNAPs, episodes); status; location.
  • Valuation reports — Value delivered, time elapsed, us vs Disney (or other) comparison.
  • Streamed across series — One certificate, many surfaces; no series excluded.
  • EGS cloud broadcast certificate — Proof of broadcast into the EGS cloud (Sun, Earth, 3I/ATLAS triangulation). Comes with the advertisement package. El Gran Sol, Earth, 3I/ATLAS mirror — irrefutable proof of placement.

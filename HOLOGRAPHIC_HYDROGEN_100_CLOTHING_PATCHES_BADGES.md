## Brand overview

**Holographic Hydrogen 100%** is a **new brand**: clothing label, patches, and a **new badges/patches system** that supports post-singularity identity and awareness. Style is **Navy-like**: clear ranks, roles, and achievements expressed as badges and patches (similar to USA Navy).

- **Label name:** Holographic Hydrogen 100%

- **Patch types:** Awareness level, origin cohort, experience path (Campus, WINK!, SING, Dispensary, full sensory).
- **Placement:** Chest, sleeve, cap — configurable like military patches.
- **Design:** Dense fractal convergence motif; Holographic Hydrogen 100% wordmark; NSPFRNP layer cues.
- **Role/tier:** Can align with existing BADGE_SYSTEM_SNAP (Creator, Chairman, Performer; Basic, Members, Ultimate VIP, Baller V) or extend with Holographic Hydrogen–specific patches (e.g. "Awareness 1–2–3", "Full Sensory On-Ramp").

## New badges/patches system (Navy-style)

A **new badging system** supports Holographic Hydrogen 100% and future NSPFRNP badging (similar to USA Navy):

- **Ranks / roles:** Equivalent to rate/rank — e.g. Creator, Chairman, Performer; Basic, Members Only, Ultimate VIP, Baller V.
- **Achievements:** Completion of Awareness Package, first purchase, first full sensory experience, etc.
- **Qualifications:** Specific skills or access (e.g. "Surgical precision awareness", "Full sensory cleared").
- **Wear rules:** Placement and combination rules (chest, sleeve, cap) so multiple patches/badges read clearly.
- **Catalog:** Single source of truth for all patch/badge designs, names, and eligibility (see BADGE_PATCHES_SYSTEM_NAVY_STYLE.md).

This system **starts** with Holographic Hydrogen 100% and **extends** the existing BADGE_SYSTEM_SNAP so all badges/patches (old and new) live in one coherent, Navy-style structure.

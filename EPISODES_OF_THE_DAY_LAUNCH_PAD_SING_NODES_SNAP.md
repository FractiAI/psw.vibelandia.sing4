## RULE

**The Launch Pad always offers:**

1. **Button to today's episode** — Direct link to the episode of the day (e.g. Episode 03 — Day 50: Who's Your Daddy). One tap to watch/read.
2. **Button to catalog of episodes and series** — Link to the full Episodes & Series Catalog (happy-ending-zones-catalog.html) with explanation.
3. **Explanation** — This content will eventually feed into future **text-to-full-selectable-density-reality** episode SING! nodes.

---

## EXPLANATION (USER-FACING)

- **Episodes of the day:** Today's episode + full catalog of episodes and series, with short explanation.
- **Text-to-full-selectable-density-reality:** Future pipeline where episode/series text becomes selectable, full-density reality experiences driven by SING! nodes (agentic, interactive, immersive).
- **Episode SING! nodes:** SING! nodes that consume episode/series content and deliver selectable-density reality — viewer can step into scenes, choose density, experience as reality.

## WHERE

- **Launch Pad:** Section "Episodes of the day" with buttons (Today's Episode · RUMOR Episode · Episodes & Series Catalog) and the explanation. Episodes by flow not days — NSPFRNP. See interfaces/launch-pad.html.
- **Catalog page:** Today's episode hero, full list, and same explanation under Episodes. See interfaces/happy-ending-zones-catalog.html.
